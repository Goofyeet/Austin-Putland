    About Me:
    I was born and raised in a small town with a population of 1000 people in Saskatchewan, Canada. After highschool, I went to college in Moose Jaw, SK for a diploma in Electrical Engineering Technology. This was a Co-op program where I gained work experience with testing and maintaining high voltage electrical equipment. After I graduated, I worked for three and a half years designing, commissioning, and troubleshooting control systems for grain terminals and potash mines. During this time, I taught myself Python to create simple programs with, and then I moved on to C++ and assembly for my IR remote project. In August of 2022, I moved to Halifax with my girlfriend as she got accepted into Dalhousie for grad school. I quickly realized I wanted to turn my hobby into a career and enrolled into a B.S in CS.
